# Distribution Runbook — Porch & Party

## Goal
Reinforce the site, services, and entity understanding outside the repo without turning distribution into random posting.

## Weekly rhythm
1. Publish or review one site page from the approved queue.
2. Mirror that page with one short social post or caption around the same service language.
3. Add one fresh image to Google Business Profile if available.

## Monthly rhythm
1. Review top service pages and FAQs.
2. Confirm legal pages, contact path, and form link still work.
3. Confirm GBP service areas and services still match the site.
4. Review whether any page should be expanded, tightened, or retired.

## Content reinforcement themes
- Seasonal porch installs
- Home birthday setup before/after
- Romantic room setups
- Grazing table styling
- Corporate event styling
- Shower setup styling

## Offsite consistency rules
- Use Porch & Party everywhere
- Use hello@porchandparty901.com as the public email
- Do not describe the business as full-service event planning unless the offer actually changes
- Keep pricing language consistent with the site: starting at, custom quote, scope-based pricing
