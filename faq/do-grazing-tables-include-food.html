Grazing FAQ

Do grazing tables include food?

Grazing-table pricing can sound straightforward until people realize some businesses are quoting styling only while others are quoting food, styling, and labor together.

Service area focus: Memphis, TN, Germantown, TN, Arlington, TN and the greater Memphis metro area.

Quick answer

No. Porch & Party grazing-table starting pricing covers styling only. Food is not included by default, but food sourcing can be requested for an additional fee.

Who this is for

This page is for hosts and organizers who need to know whether the visible grazing-table price includes food before they submit a quote request.

What this includes

  • A clear distinction between styling and food sourcing
  • A quote path for adding food when needed
  • Pricing clarity before the lead reaches the form

Grazing Tables & Styled Setups starts at $250+. Base pricing covers styling only. Food is not included by default and can be sourced for an additional fee.

What this means in practice

This distinction protects expectations. Styling-only covers the visual presentation and setup support, while food sourcing changes cost, logistics, and overall event planning needs.

Why this page exists

This page exists because unclear food language is one of the fastest ways to create confusion and bad-fit leads for grazing-table inquiries.
