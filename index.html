Common Questions

Quick answers before you reach out.

Do you decorate hotel rooms and private homes?

Yes. Porch & Party offers celebration setups for both hotel rooms and private homes across the Memphis metro area.

Do you provide food for grazing tables?

Base grazing-table pricing covers styling only. Food is not included by default, but food sourcing can be requested for an additional fee.

How far in advance should I book?

Earlier is better, especially for seasonal installs and weekends, but reach out with your date and Porch & Party will confirm availability.

Do you offer take-down for seasonal porch decorating?

Yes. Optional take-down service is available for an additional fee.
